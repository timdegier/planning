The reconsidered model

Right now every show is treated as real, booked spend. To plan "what can I still do within budget," I'd introduce a status on every line:

  • Confirmed — booked shows with real cost + first-listen forecasts (your current 27).
  • Placeholder — tentative blocks you pencil in to use up remaining room. They carry a cost (and maybe an FL estimate), but they're visually distinct (dashed/ghosted) and clearly not committed.

The budget footer then stops being one "spend" number and becomes a waterfall:

Budget → − Confirmed → − Placeholder → = Vrije ruimte (still unallocated)

So per month and in total you'd see: this much is locked, this much is penciled in, this much is genuinely free. That directly answers "what can I do within the budget."
